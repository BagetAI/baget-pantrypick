Shelf-life Decay Mapping Rules

When receipts are processed, fresh food decays programmatically on the following default values:

  • Fresh berries, cut greens: 3-4 days
  • Fresh poultry, meats: 3 days
  • Vegetables, root crops: 7-14 days
  • Pantry dry staples: 180 days

Verification Mechanism Rules

To maintain high parent engagement and avoid the typical 90% abandonment of manual logs, PantryPick utilizes a streamlined, single-tap confirmation checklist.

1. Instant OCR & NLP Mapping

Once raw item strings are captured (e.g., "ORGC SPNCH 10OZ"), NLP maps them to readable items ("Organic Spinach"), pre-tags estimated shelf life, and tags exact weight.

2. Single-Tap Pruning

Parents can quickly verify estimated expirations, delete false parses, and increment counts via simple rounded touch-targets (min 44px) without keyboard input.
